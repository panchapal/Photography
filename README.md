## 🚀 Features
<br/>🎨 Interactive Animations: Engaging CSS animations and interactive elements.
<br/>📸 Dynamic Content: JavaScript-powered dynamic display of photos and videos.
<br/>🖼 Gallery Showcase: A visually appealing gallery to display my best work.
<br/>🎥 Video Section: Embedded videos to showcase cinematic wedding highlights.
<br/>💬 Testimonials: Client reviews and feedback to build trust and credibility.
<br/>📩 Contact Form: A functional form for visitors to get in touch with me.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- HTML5: Structuring the content.
- CSS3: Styling and animations.
- JavaScript: Dynamic content and interactivity.
- jQuery: Simplifies DOM manipulation and event handling.
- Font Awesome: Beautiful icons.
- Google Fonts: Custom fonts for a unique design.
- YouTube Embed: Showcasing cinematic wedding videos.
- Google Maps: Displaying location in the contact section.

## 📖 Usage
<br/>🏠 Home Section – A welcoming introduction with a brief description of the portfolio.
<br/>🙋‍♂️ About Section – Learn more about me, my journey, and my passion for photography.
<br/>🖼 Gallery Section: Explore my best work, including wedding, engagement, and love story photos.
<br/>🎥 Video Section: Watch cinematic wedding highlights and teasers.
<br/>💬 Testimonials: Read what my clients have to say about my work.
<br/>📩 Contact Section – A form allowing visitors to reach out directly.
